### Tomorrows

Her advisor gave her an opportunity for some hands-on experience. Go to this village, build rapport, document. 

They put her on the boat. The Atlantic still carried the same smell on the same but different spindrift. The catch had been bad, nor did the children stay here — not when they could live in the city and do work that does not shear the body and eat sardines from a can with a self-similar paint job.

"It's the way things are."

The catch never was good, but the catch never was insufficient.

The village chapel held the notebook. She cut open the fish and removed the guts and scaled and in ten minutes the smell of fish settled beneath her skin.

Salt, barrel, nailed exactly so. Twenty-eight days.

It was another woman's method. It is to be remembered by doing.
